## Caveats

- ACCESS_HISTORY lags up to ~3h and QUERY_ATTRIBUTION_HISTORY up to ~8h (UTC);
  today's rows are always partial until the daily 06:45 task settles them.
- The daily task (`TASK_LOAD_OBJECT_COST`, 3-day window) needs no changes —
  it calls the swapped proc and re-derives the trailing window every morning.
